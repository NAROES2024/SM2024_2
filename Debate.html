Ocean Debates

The Ocean Matters debate is an engaging event that aims to stimulate critical thinking, research skills, and public speaking abilities among participants. It provides a platform for lively discussions and debates on important ocean-related topics, encouraging participants to delve into the complexities of marine conservation and explore various perspectives.

Stages and Timeline

There are 2 rounds as difficulty level increases,and also a final round(surprise round).

Stage 1: Team Formation

1. Team Formation: Participants can form teams consisting of a specified number of members (e.g., 3 or 4 members per team) who will represent different viewpoints on the given topics.
2. Topic Selection: Prior to the event, a list of ocean-related topics will be provided, covering various aspects of marine conservation, oceanography, and environmental issues.

Rounds

3. Preparation Time: Each team will be given a designated period to research and prepare their arguments and evidence supporting their assigned viewpoint on the chosen topic.
4. Debate Rounds: The event will consist of multiple debate rounds, each focusing on a specific topic. Each round will include constructive speeches, rebuttals, and a concluding statement.
5. Moderation: A moderator will ensure fair and respectful participation, manage time limits, and maintain the flow of the debate.

Judging Panel and Criteria

Participants will be evaluated based on the following criteria:
1. Knowledge and Research(15 Marks) : Depth of understanding, factual accuracy, and evidence-based arguments related to the chosen topic.
2. Communication and Presentation(10 Marks) : Clarity, persuasiveness, and effective delivery of arguments through logical reasoning, examples, and supporting evidence.
3. Rebuttal and Counter Arguments(15 Marks): Ability to respond to opposing arguments with well-articulated counterpoints and refutations.
4. Engagement and Participation(5 Marks): Active involvement in the debate, respect for others' opinions, and ability to engage the audience.
5. Overall Impact(5 Marks): Coherence of arguments, ability to present a compelling case, and demonstration of critical thinking skills.

Problem Statement


The participants have to select a real world ship/ferry and create a model of it. You will have to present your design ideas compulsorily including the following parameters:
1. Physical parameters including length, breadth, draft etc.
2. Propulsion and control mechanism.
3. Type of hull form.
4. Real life application and uses.
5. Any other special feature/ innovation.

Minimum size of the model is
Length = 10cm
Breadth = 5 cm
Depth = 2.5 cm

While there is no upper size limit to the model it is recommended that the model is easy to transport.

Rules and Regulations

1. Language and Style: This debate will be conducted in the English language only, and we expect all participants to engage in a polite and respectable manner throughout the event.
2. Respectful Listening: While your opponent team is presenting their arguments, we urge all participants to maintain calmness and refrain from interrupting in the middle of their speech. Please give them the opportunity to express their ideas fully.
3. No Interruptions: Breaking the rule of interrupting your opponent's speech will result in losing your chance to speak during that specific segment. Let's show courtesy to our fellow debaters.
4. Preparation Time: Once the debate topic is revealed, teams will be given a fair preparation time of 5 to 10 minutes. Feel free to jot down your main points on a piece of paper during this period.
5. No Phones: To maintain focus and fairness, the use of phones will not be allowed after the preparation time. Please refrain from using them during the debate.
6. Respectful Tone: Shouting or using aggressive language is not allowed. Engage in discussions with a composed and respectful tone. Teams that violate this rule may receive negative marks.
7. Keep Calm and Polite: Above all, let's remember to be calm, cool, and polite throughout the debate. We're here to learn from each other's perspectives and engage in constructive conversations.

Prizes & Rewards

Boat Expo 2023 will award cash prizes to the top 3 participating teams based on the presentation made. The overall prize pool money is ₹15,000/- for this event. The winners will also receive Certificates from Samudramanthan and a mention on our social media pages.
