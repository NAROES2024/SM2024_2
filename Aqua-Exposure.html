Aqua-Exposure

Aqua-Exposure is an interactive exhibition showcasing marine life and underwater ecosystems.

Aqua-Exposure

This event has the primary objective of shedding light on the profound significance and captivating allure inherent in the realms of ocean engineering and naval architecture. Through the presentation of photographs that are not only visually enthralling but also distinctively original, the event strives to underscore the vital roles these fields play in our world. Moreover, it endeavors to foster a sense of ethical responsibility among photographers, emphasizing the importance of maintaining integrity and authenticity in their artistic pursuits.By seamlessly blending the artistic vision with the ethical imperative, this event aspires to create a platform where the beauty and value of ocean engineering and naval architecture are celebrated while upholding the highest standards of creative and professional integrity.

Stages and Timeline

This is a single stage competition in which participant have to submi their photo graphs in the given google form and Participants must upload their photo graphs to their Instagram account .

Each participant can submit a maximum of three photographs.

The images must be original and captured by the participant before the event's deadline.

Judging Panel & Criteria

The number of likes and shares on Instagram will be the primary factor in determining the winners:
1. Adherence to the theme. (15 marks)
2. Inclusion of all requirements stated above (30 marks)
3. Originality and creative expression (40 marks)
4. Choice of design scheme (15 marks)

Rules and Regulations

1. The contest is open to individuals of all ages and backgrounds.

2. Participants must possess an Instagram account to enter the contest.

3. Photos that have been previously submitted to other contests or plagiarized images are not allowed.

4.Participants must include the official event hashtag, such as #OceanEngPhotoContest, in the caption of their photo. Additionally, participants should tag the official event Instagram account (@OceanEngPhotoContest) in the image itself.

5. Image Quality and Specifications: Photographs should be of high quality and resolution. Images must be in JPEG format and should not exceed 10 MB in size. Avoid heavy editing or manipulation that distorts the original subject matter.

6. Copyright and Ownership: Participants retain full copyright ownership of their images. Engaging in plagiarism within the context of the photography competition will lead to automatic disqualification.

Prizes & Rewards

All participants will receive certificate of participation from Samudra Manthan, IIT Kharagpur.

Capture the Water will award cash prizes to the top 3 participants on the basis of the mentioned criteria. The overall prize money is ₹6000/- for this event. The winners will also receive Certificate from Samudra Manthan and a mention on our social media pages.
