Capture the Water

Are you a photography enthusiast looking for an opportunity to showcase your skills and win amazing prizes? Look no further than "Capture the Moment", the photography competition that challenges you to capture life's fleeting moments and transform them into art. Whether you prefer to capture the beauty of nature, the energy of the city, or the emotions of people, this competition is the perfect platform to showcase your talent. Submit your best photographs and compete against fellow photography enthusiasts for the chance to win prizes that will take your photography to the next level. We welcome entries from photographers of all levels, from amateur to professional. So grab your camera and join us in this exciting competition. Capture the Moment and let your creativity shine!

Stages and Timeline

The event will be organized in 1 round. The participants will have to capture the photographs or videos and edit it into a video for judging. The video with the maximum number of points will eventually win the competition.

Stage 1

Participants have to capture original photographs and videos of the waters, ships, or anything related to the marine background and edit it into a video of 30 seconds, which is the maximum limit of the video. Artwork should not be taken from the internet or faked as this might result in disqualification from the competition.

Stage 2

This is a one round event.

Problem Statement

There is no problem statement for this event.

Judging Panel & Criteria

1.The participants will be judged based on how elegant and lively the photograph or video looks.
2.Following the time limit.
3.Originality of the artwork.
4.Editing style and attractiveness of the video.
5.The selection of the background music.

Rules and Regulations

1.This is an individual event.

2.Each entry should be an authentic and original work of the participants.

3.Plagiarism is strongly discouraged, and anyone found indulging in unfair practices will be summarily removed from the event. If the violation is found to be serious, they may also be removed from Samudramanthan 2023.

4.If there is any question about authenticity, Samudramanthan, IIT Kharagpur reserves the right to demand proof of authorship. If it is not then your entry will be disqualified.

5.Watermark of the editing software should not be present in the video.

6.The rules, regulations, and modifications to this event will be released on the Samudramanthan official website at https://www.samudramanthan.in The decision of the judges is final and binding on all participant

Prizes & Rewards

All participating teams will receive certificates of participation from Samudramanthan, IIT Kharagpur. The top submissions may be published on our social media pages to appreciate their unique designs and design approach.

Innotect 2021 will award cash prizes to the top 3 participating teams on the basis of the presentation made in round 2. The overall prize money is ₹ 15,000/- for this event. The winners will also receive e-Certificates from Samudramanthan and a mention on our social media pages.
