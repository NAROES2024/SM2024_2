Stages and Timeline

This is a three-stage competition with an increase in level and an increase in the difficulty of the clues given by the team.
The winner in each pool will be provided with a reward.

Stage 1: Guess the word

Consists of a single puzzle whose answer will be the password of the next stage.

Stage 2:

The clues and hints will be given in a pdf the password of which is the answer of stage 1.

Problem Statement

There is no problem statement for this event.
The participants will be given a scrambled cube and they will be needed to find out a pattern.

Judging Panel & Criteria

The winner will be awarded as the first player who solves all of the riddles and completes the treasure hunt in their specific pool.

Rules and Regulations

1. Destroying any clue will lead to disqualification.

2. Any kind of misbehavior will lead to immediate Disqualification.

3. The event organizers will not be responsible for any kind of technical issue during the treasure hunt event.

4. All participants must follow the rules and play fairly. Any participant found to be cheating, vandalizing, or disrupting the event in any way will be immediately disqualified.

5. The participant must bring their laptop.

6.Participant must bring their laptop.

Prizes & Rewards

Each participant will get a certificate of participation from Samudra Manthan, IIT Kharagpur.

The participant who completes all three stages of cryptic clues by finding the answers will be awarded a 2000 INR cash Prize from Samudra Manthan, IIT Kharagpur.
